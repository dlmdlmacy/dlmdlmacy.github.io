Orange Dormitary

Dormitary

The internal facilities of the dormitory are complete.

There are four people in each dormitory.

At the same time, the dormitory is equipped with hot kettle and air conditioner.

There is no power cut in the graduate dormitory for 24 hours.

There is a laundry room on the first floor of the dormitory auxiliary building.

Orange Dining Hall

Dining Hall

Food is the first and foremost sustenance in people’s lives.

There are three canteens in our school, each with its own characteristics. Graduate students mainly live in the orange garden, the most often patronized is the orange garden canteen next to the dormitory.

The dining hall is divided into two floors. There are many delicious foods. You can always find one that suits your taste.

There are supermarket, barber shop, fruit shop and so on.

Crown Gymnasium

Indoor Stadium

There are badminton, basketball, volleyball, table tennis, dance, fitness, martial arts, taekwondo and other venues in the gymnasium.

You can make an appointment in the app of Southeast University or the personal information portal. The appointment time is 8:00-22:00, and you can make an appointment for nearly 3 days.

After the appointment is successful, you must swipe the card on time, otherwise you will be punished for breach of trust.

Express Delivery

Express

In the era of Internet plus, express delivery is an essential part of our contemporary college students' life.

The Juyuan dormitory is equipped with the west gate nearest neighbor express receiving point, and the opening hours are 9:00am ~ 7:30pm.
